About Me

Hi there! I'm Mayur Nandkishor Phatanagre, a passionate Artificial Intelligence developer with a strong background in physics and data science. My journey into AI began with a curiosity to explore the endless possibilities of this rapidly evolving field.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Physics, and I've furthered my education with a Post-Graduation Diploma in Data Science and Machine Learning. I've earned certifications in Python, Data Science, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. These learning experiences have equipped me with a solid foundation to tackle complex AI challenges.

During my professional journey, I completed two internships that provided me hands-on experience. In my first internship, I worked on image processing and machine learning tasks, gaining practical insights into data processing and analysis. In the second internship, I collaborated with cross-functional teams to develop a computer vision module for vehicle registration number detection and retention forecasting. This experience honed my skills in real-world AI applications.

As an Artificial Intelligence developer at Quantum Technology, I've delved into projects involving face recognition, face detection, and object tracking. I've developed and maintained deep learning models using tools such as TensorFlow, Keras, and OpenCV.I've also delved into the world of Raspberry Pi, developing AI models for real-world applications. My passion for AI is fueled by its potential to revolutionize industries and enhance everyday life.
